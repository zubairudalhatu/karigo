# Controlled Soft Launch Approval Checklist

## Product

- [x] Core mock-provider flows and dashboards work
- [ ] Physical-device/browser pilot stability signed off
- [ ] Support, refund, and dispatch processes rehearsed

## Operations

- [ ] Initial vendors and riders confirmed/onboarded
- [ ] Support, dispatch, and finance officers assigned
- [ ] Daily reporting and incident response rehearsed

## Legal And Policy

- [ ] Terms, privacy policy, and refund policy approved
- [ ] Vendor and rider agreements approved
- [ ] Data-protection/security review completed

## Provider And Infrastructure Readiness

- [ ] Production infrastructure, monitoring, backup/restore, HTTPS, and CORS approved
- [ ] Payment provider selected; sandbox and reconciliation completed
- [ ] SMS OTP provider selected; delivery and distributed rate limits completed
- [ ] Live keys secured and provider dashboards access-controlled
- [ ] Webhook signature/idempotency and admin controls verified
- [ ] Vendor/admin production session hardening completed

## Decision

- [ ] Ready for controlled soft launch
- [x] Ready only for internal demo and staging preparation
- [x] Not ready for real-customer soft launch due to open blockers

Approval requires named evidence and sign-off from Product, Engineering, Operations,
Finance, Security, and Legal.
