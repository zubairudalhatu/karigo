# Customer Mobile App

The Customer App is the consumer surface for KariGO ordering and discovery.

## Current Task 44 Experience

- KariGO branded red top bar on Home, Browse, Cart, Orders and Profile.
- Category-first Home screen with no individual product feed.
- Vendor-first Browse screen with products shown only after vendor sections.
- Vendor storefronts are the primary product-discovery surface.
- Persistent bottom navigation uses proper icons and text labels.
- Public vendor application entry is available from Profile.
