# Investor And Partner Due-Diligence Checklist

Use approved, current documents only. Apply access controls and confidentiality rules;
do not share credentials, customer data, private source code, or unapproved projections.

## Corporate

- [ ] Certificate of incorporation and corporate profile
- [ ] Directors/management information
- [ ] Verified corporate bank details and tax information
- [ ] Shareholding/capital structure where applicable

## Product

- [x] MVP/product overview and controlled demo access plan
- [x] Architecture/API documentation and product roadmap references
- [x] Security/readiness summary and known limitations
- [ ] Approved external security/privacy assessment

## Commercial

- [ ] Approved business plan, investor teaser, and pitch deck
- [ ] Revenue model and financial assumptions
- [ ] Funding requirement and approved use of funds
- [ ] Unit-economics assumptions with source evidence

## Operations

- [x] Vendor/rider onboarding, support, dispatch, refund, settlement, and pilot workflows documented
- [ ] Real pilot results and approved operational SLAs

## Legal

- [ ] Approved Terms, Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, Vendor Agreement, Rider Agreement
- [ ] Data-protection review and intellectual-property ownership evidence
