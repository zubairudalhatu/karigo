# Taxi Readiness Note

Taxi is visible on the Customer App homepage as a readiness-gated service only.

When tapped, customers see:

`Taxi is coming soon. KariGO is preparing verified driver onboarding, fare controls, maps, safety checks and dispatch operations before launch.`

## Not Live Yet

Task 53 does not implement:

- Live taxi ride requests
- Driver dispatch
- Fare calculation
- Map tracking
- Driver onboarding approval
- Safety checks
- Taxi payment flow
- Integration with the Rider App delivery dispatch workflow

## Future Dependencies

Taxi should not go live until KariGO approves:

- Driver onboarding and verification process
- Vehicle and safety requirements
- Fare control model
- Dispatch operations
- Customer support and incident process
- Insurance/legal review where applicable
- Staging pilot and operational sign-off

Taxi must remain separate from the existing delivery Rider workflow until a dedicated ride-hailing operating model is approved.
