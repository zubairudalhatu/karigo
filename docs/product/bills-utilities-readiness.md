# Bills & Utilities Readiness

Bills & Utilities is visible in the Customer App as a readiness-gated section only.

## Services Listed

- Airtime
- Data
- Electricity
- Cable TV

Each tile shows `Coming soon` and routes to a controlled readiness screen:

`Bills & Utilities is coming soon. KariGO is preparing secure provider integrations for airtime, data, electricity and cable TV payments.`

## Not Live Yet

Task 53 does not implement:

- Airtime purchase
- Data bundle purchase
- Electricity token purchase
- Cable TV subscription payment
- Live provider checkout
- Wallet funding or wallet payment
- Provider secrets or production credentials
- Payment reconciliation for bills

## Future Dependencies

Before launch, KariGO needs:

- Approved provider API selection
- Sandbox test credentials
- Server-side transaction verification
- Customer receipt and support workflow
- Refund/reversal policy
- Provider failure handling
- Admin reporting and reconciliation
- Legal and operational approval

Until then, the section is a product-navigation placeholder and education point only.
