# KariGO Soft Launch Master Message - Task 105

Date prepared: 2026-07-13

## One-Line Message

KariGO is beginning controlled early access in Kano.

## Short Paragraph

KariGO connects customers, vendors and Delivery Captains for food, groceries, market
essentials, parcel delivery and SME Services. Early access is limited to selected Kano
users while the team tests operations, support and service quality carefully.

## Longer Announcement

KariGO is beginning controlled early access in Kano. During this phase, selected customers
will be able to order food, groceries and market essentials, send parcels and request SME
Services through KariGO's connected platform.

The pilot brings together customers, local vendors, KariGO operations and Delivery
Captains in a monitored rollout. The goal is to test real ordering, vendor response,
delivery coordination, support handling and customer feedback before wider availability.

Some services are still in pilot, test mode, or readiness-only and will be activated
gradually after approval.

## Core Message

KariGO connects customers, vendors and Delivery Captains for food, groceries, market
essentials, parcel delivery and SME Services.

## Brand Tone Guide

- Clear and confident.
- Friendly and practical for Kano users.
- Honest about controlled early access.
- Local, helpful and operationally grounded.
- No hype about services that are not live.
- No promise of instant refunds, automated payouts, wallet withdrawals, live rides or live
  utility fulfilment.
