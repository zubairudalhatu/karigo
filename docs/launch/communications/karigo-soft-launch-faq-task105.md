# KariGO Soft Launch FAQ - Task 105

Date prepared: 2026-07-13

## 1. What is KariGO?

KariGO is a delivery and local-commerce platform connecting customers, vendors and
Delivery Captains for food, groceries, market essentials, parcel delivery and SME
Services.

## 2. Is KariGO fully launched?

Not yet. KariGO is beginning controlled early access in Kano with selected users. Wider
public launch will come later after testing, approval and operational review.

## 3. Where is KariGO available now?

KariGO early access is focused on selected areas in Kano.

## 4. What can I order?

During early access, selected users may test food delivery, groceries and market
essentials, parcel delivery and SME Services where available.

## 5. Are rides available?

No. KariGO Rides is readiness-only during this phase. Live ride trips are not active.

## 6. Are utility payments available?

No. Utilities are not live during controlled early access. Accelerate.ng is a future
utility integration concept and will require separate approval and testing.

## 7. How do I become a vendor?

Use the KariGO vendor application page or the approved vendor onboarding link:
[VENDOR_APPLICATION_LINK]. The team will review applications before onboarding.

## 8. How do I become a Delivery Captain?

Use the approved KariGO Captain onboarding or application link:
[CAPTAIN_APPLICATION_LINK]. Delivery Captain participation is reviewed before access is
granted.

## 9. How do payments work during early access?

Payment handling depends on the approved pilot mode. Mock payment may be used for staging
tests. Paystack Test Mode may be used only for approved verification. Live public payment
collection is not active unless separately approved in the future.

## 10. What is Paystack Test Mode?

Paystack Test Mode is a controlled testing setup for payment verification. It is not live
public payment collection. Do not share card details, payment credentials or provider
screenshots in support chats.

## 11. Can I use wallet?

KariGO Wallet is a foundation feature during early access. Wallet top-up, withdrawal and
automatic wallet refunds are not live.

## 12. How do refunds work?

Refund questions are reviewed by KariGO operations during the pilot. Do not expect instant
automatic refunds. Support may ask for a safe order reference, but never your password,
OTP, delivery code or card details.

## 13. How do I report an issue?

## 14. How do I give feedback?

Use the pilot feedback link shared by KariGO:
[FEEDBACK_LINK_PLACEHOLDER]. Please do not include passwords, OTPs, delivery codes or

## 15. When will KariGO expand?

KariGO will expand gradually after pilot results, operational readiness, management
approval and safety reviews.
