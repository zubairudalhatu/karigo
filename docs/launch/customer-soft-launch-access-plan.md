# Customer Soft Launch Access Plan

## Access

Use invite-only access for a small, known first group: staff, trusted community members,
partner/vendor contacts, and selected Kano residents within approved zones. Do not
advertise open public access. Record invitation owner, zone, and feedback consent.

Promo-code-based entry may support measurement, but it must not replace access controls.
Confirm service zones/hours, support contact, and payment mode before every invitation.

## Customer Message

> KariGO is currently in controlled soft launch in selected Kano areas. Some services
> may be limited while we test and improve the platform.

Explain whether the pilot uses mock, sandbox, or an approved manual payment process.
Never imply mock payment is a real charge. Provide the approved support contact and
feedback form after delivery.

## Feedback And Control

Support records complaints/tickets; Operations records service feedback and incidents;
Management reviews daily metrics. Pause invitations immediately when support, rider, or
vendor capacity exceeds agreed limits.
