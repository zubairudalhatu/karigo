# Public Launch Communication Plan

## Audiences And Channels

| Audience | Primary message | Channels |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Customers | Reliable local delivery in approved Kano zones | Website, social, flyers, approved email/WhatsApp |
| Vendors | Reach customers through controlled fulfilment tools | Direct outreach, posters, social |
| Riders | Verified delivery opportunities and transparent workflow | Recruitment materials, direct onboarding |
| SMEs/corporate users | Approved service-provider requests and local logistics | Direct sales, website, email |
| Media/community | KariGO launch scope, local value, safety/support | Press/community brief, social |
| Internal team | Scope, roles, escalation, launch-day decisions | Briefing, operations channels |

Do not use WhatsApp/email/push publicly until consent/provider approval exists.

Approved channel plan must explicitly cover the website, social media, WhatsApp
broadcast, email, flyers, vendor posters, rider recruitment materials, and an optional
press announcement. Record the owner, audience, consent basis, approval, schedule, and
success measure for every enabled channel.

## Draft Messages

**Customer:** KariGO is launching local delivery in selected Kano areas. Order from
approved vendors, track progress, and reach our support team when you need help.

**Vendor:** Join KariGO to receive and manage local customer orders. Approved pilot
vendors receive onboarding, training, and operational support.

**Rider:** Apply to join KariGO's verified rider network. Complete verification, safety
training, and app onboarding before activation.

**SME/corporate:** KariGO supports selected local service-provider requests and delivery
needs for Kano businesses. Contact our team for approved service coverage.

**Offer:** Use the approved KariGO launch offer within its published terms and limits.

**Support:** KariGO Support is available through the approved contact during published
operating hours.

Tone: clear, friendly, local, trustworthy, and action-driven. Marketing/Legal must approve
claims, prices, zones, provider references, offer terms, and support details.
