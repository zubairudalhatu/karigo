# Production Support Playbook

| Category | First response | Required information | Internal action | Escalation | Target | Closure note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Order delay | We are checking your order and will update you shortly. | Order ref, latest status, contact availability | Check vendor/rider/dispatch timeline | Operations/Dispatch | First response 15 min | Cause, update, outcome |
| Payment issue | We have recorded the payment issue for review. | Order/payment ref, time, approved evidence; never PIN/OTP | Verify backend/provider/reconciliation | Finance + Technical | 15 min; decision 4 hrs | Reconciled result |
| Refund request | Your refund request is under review. | Order/payment ref, reason | Use controlled refund workflow/policy | Finance/Admin | 30 min; policy SLA | Approval/denial and reference |
| Wrong item | We are sorry; we are reviewing the incorrect item with the vendor. | Order ref, expected/received item, safe evidence | Verify item and vendor fulfilment; assess replacement/refund | Vendor Ops/Finance | 15 min; resolve same day | Remedy and vendor action |
| Missing item | We are sorry; we are checking the missing item with the vendor. | Order ref, missing item, package details | Verify order items and handoff; assess remedy/refund | Vendor Ops/Finance | 15 min; resolve same day | Missing item outcome |
| Damaged item | We are sorry; we are reviewing the damaged item. | Order ref, item, safe photo/evidence | Verify damage and custody; assess remedy/refund | Vendor Ops/Finance | 15 min; resolve same day | Remedy and responsibility |
| Vendor rejection | The vendor could not fulfil this order; we are reviewing next steps. | Order ref/reason/payment | Validate rejection and refund need | Ops/Finance | 15 min | Customer informed/refund path |
| Rider delay | Dispatch is following up with your rider. | Order ref/status/location context | Contact/reassign/escalate | Dispatch/Ops | 10 min | Delivery/reassignment result |
| Failed delivery | We are reviewing why delivery could not be completed. | Order ref, attempts, contact notes | Secure item; decide retry/return/refund | Operations/Finance | Immediate | Custody and final disposition |
| Login issue | We will help verify the account securely. | Phone, error/time; never password/OTP | Check status/OTP/provider logs | Technical | 15 min | Access restored or next step |
| Promo issue | We are checking the offer terms and your order eligibility. | Code/order/ref/error | Validate promo rules/usage | Product/Admin | 30 min | Eligibility explained |
| App issue | Thank you; our technical team is reviewing it. | App, version, device, steps, screenshot without secrets | Reproduce/log/triage | Technical | Severity-based | Fix/workaround/version |

Internal notes must remain private. Safety, security, duplicate charges, data exposure,
or widespread outages escalate immediately under the launch escalation matrix.
