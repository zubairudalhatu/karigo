Date prepared: 2026-07-15

## Purpose

Assign ownership for Batch 1 issues and fixes so operations, support, technical
and management teams know who is accountable for each follow-up.

This tracker must not contain credentials, OTPs, delivery codes, full private
contact details, APK links, tokens, payment secrets, bank details or unmasked
screenshots.

| Role | Responsibility |
