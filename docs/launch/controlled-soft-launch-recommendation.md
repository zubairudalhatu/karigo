# Controlled Soft Launch Recommendation

## Recommendation

Do **not** start a real-customer controlled soft launch yet. Continue with internal demo,
private staging deployment, and a full staging E2E simulation first.

## Recommended Pilot Duration

After blockers close, run a controlled Kano pilot for **2 to 4 weeks**.

## Recommended Kano Zones

Start with a smaller high-control subset before expanding:

- Nasarawa GRA
- Bompai
- Zoo Road
- Tarauni
- Farm Centre

Expand to additional documented zones only after daily performance is stable.

## Recommended Starting Capacity

- Maximum vendors at start: 3 to 5 verified vendors
- Maximum riders at start: 5 to 8 trained riders plus reserve coverage
- Customer access method: invite-only controlled group
- Support operating hours: approved pilot hours with named support, dispatch, finance,
  operations, and technical escalation owners

## Recommended Provider Mode At Pilot Start

- Internal demo: mock providers
- Private staging: mock providers plus approved sandbox providers only
- Real-customer soft launch: live providers only after formal approval, or a documented
  manual/mock payment operating policy if management explicitly limits the pilot to a
  closed non-commercial test

Do not activate live payment, SMS, email, WhatsApp, push, or bank transfer providers from
this recommendation alone.

## Expansion Criteria

Expand only when:

- Primary staging scenario passes.
- Critical negative scenarios pass.
- Order completion rate meets management target.
- Vendor acceptance rate meets management target.
- Rider coverage is sufficient for selected zones.
- Support queue and refund requests remain manageable.
- No critical technical/security issue is open.
- Daily reporting is reviewed by management.

## Pause Or Rollback Criteria

Pause or roll back the pilot if:

- Payment verification or refund control fails.
- OTP/security/authentication fails.
- Customers can access another customer's data.
- Vendors or riders can access another party's records.
- Orders cannot progress through vendor, dispatch, rider, and completion states.
- Support cannot respond within the approved SLA.
- A serious safety, privacy, or reputational incident occurs.

## Management Decisions Required Before Launch

- Approve pilot zones and capacity.
- Approve vendors/riders/customer invite model.
- Approve legal/policy documents.
- Approve provider mode and payment/OTP approach.
- Approve pilot budget and operating team roles.
- Approve incident escalation and refund process.
- Approve go/no-go recommendation after staging evidence.
