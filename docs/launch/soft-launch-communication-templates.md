# Soft Launch Communication Templates

Use approved support contacts and channels. Do not promise unavailable services or expose
internal details.

**Customer invitation:**  
KariGO is inviting a limited group to our controlled Kano soft launch. Services are
available only in selected areas while we test and improve. We will share your approved
access and support details separately.

**Vendor onboarding:**  
Welcome to the KariGO pilot. Our Operations team will confirm your products, operating
hours, training, test order, and support contact before activation.

**Rider onboarding:**  
Welcome to the KariGO pilot. Complete verification, app training, safety briefing, and a
test delivery before going online.

**Order issue acknowledgement:**  
We are sorry about the issue with your KariGO order. Our team is reviewing it and will
update you as soon as possible.

**Vendor delay:**  
Your KariGO order is taking longer than expected at the vendor. We are following up and
will keep you informed.

**Rider delay:**  
Your KariGO delivery is delayed. Our dispatch team is following up and will update you.

**Refund review:**  
Your refund request has been received and is under review. We will update you after the
required checks are complete.

**Support acknowledgement:**  
Thank you for contacting KariGO Support. Your request has been recorded and assigned for
review.

**Feedback request:**  
Thank you for joining the KariGO controlled soft launch. Please share your experience so
we can improve before wider launch.

**Pilot thank-you:**  
Thank you for helping KariGO test and improve local delivery in Kano. Your feedback is
helping us prepare for the next stage.
