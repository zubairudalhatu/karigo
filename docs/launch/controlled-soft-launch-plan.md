# Controlled Soft Launch Plan

## Purpose

Run a limited, governed Kano pilot to validate end-to-end ordering, vendor response,
rider performance, dispatch/admin control, customer support, complaints/refunds, and
daily management decisions before public launch.

This plan is preparation only. Real-customer traffic remains blocked until
`docs/handover/controlled-soft-launch-approval-checklist.md` is fully signed off.

## Recommended Scope

- Kano only; begin with selected high-priority zones.
- Small verified vendor and rider groups with named operations owners.
- Invite-only customers and controlled operating hours.
- Mock/sandbox payment only until the approved real payment/refund provider is certified.
- Manual support, dispatch, finance, and management oversight.
- Suggested duration: 2 to 4 weeks after approval.

## Success Criteria

- End-to-end orders complete reliably without critical security/payment incidents.
- Vendors accept/respond within agreed targets; rejection reasons are controlled.
- Riders accept, progress, and complete with OTP correctly.
- Support and refund complaints meet agreed response/resolution targets.
- Admin dashboard/reports support daily decisions and reconciliation.
- Critical incidents are escalated, documented, resolved, and reviewed.

## Daily Governance

Operations produces the daily report, reviews incidents/feedback/KPIs with management,
assigns corrective actions, and records a continue/pause/expand decision. Expansion is
forbidden until the controlled expansion criteria are met.
