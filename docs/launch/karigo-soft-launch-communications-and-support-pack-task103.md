# KariGO Soft Launch Communications And Support Pack - Task 103

Date prepared: 2026-07-13

## Purpose

Provide safe pilot communications and support responses for controlled soft launch
operations. These templates are manual. They do not send SMS, email, WhatsApp, push
notifications or any automated provider message.

## Pilot Customer Invitation

Hello, you are invited to test KariGO during a controlled Kano pilot. KariGO lets you
order from selected vendors and track delivery with approved operations support. Some
services are still in test or readiness mode, so please share feedback after each order.

## Vendor Pilot Confirmation

Hello, your business has been selected for KariGO's controlled pilot. Please keep your
dashboard open during the agreed test window, accept only available orders and update
preparation status promptly. KariGO operations will support dispatch and issue handling.

## Delivery Captain Pilot Confirmation

Hello, you are confirmed as a KariGO Delivery Captain for the controlled pilot. Please use
the KariGO Captain App during the agreed test window, go online only when available, and
complete delivery only with the customer-supplied delivery code.

## Support Acknowledgement

Thank you for contacting KariGO support. We have received your request and will review it
with the operations team. Please keep your order or request reference available.

## Order Delay Response

We are checking your order status with the vendor and Delivery Captain. We will update you
as soon as the next step is confirmed.

## Payment Test Mode Response

KariGO payment testing is controlled during staging. If your test payment could not be
verified, please retry only through the KariGO app and do not share payment details in chat
or screenshots.

## SME Services Response

Your SME Services request has been received for review. KariGO operations will assess the
request and update you in the app. Provider private contact details are not shared during
the pilot.

## Wallet And Referral Response

KariGO Wallet and Referral Rewards are currently pilot foundations. Wallet funding,
withdrawals, automatic refunds and automatic rewards are not live. Any approved adjustment
or reward review is handled by KariGO Admin.

## Pilot Feedback Request

Thank you for testing KariGO. Please share what worked well, what felt unclear and any
issue you experienced. Do not include passwords, OTPs, delivery codes, payment details or
private account information in your feedback.

## Support Escalation Paths

| Issue type | First responder | Escalation owner | Target response |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Customer question | Support Officer | Operations Lead | 15 minutes |
| Vendor delay | Support Officer | Operations Lead | 15 minutes |
| Delivery Captain delay | Dispatch Officer | Operations Lead | 10 minutes |
| Payment test issue | Support Officer | Finance/Technical Lead | 20 minutes |
| Login/session issue | Support Officer | Technical Lead | 20 minutes |
| Delivery code issue | Dispatch Officer | Technical Lead | Immediate |
| Safety/privacy concern | Any responder | Management/Technical Lead | Immediate |

## Communication Guardrails

- Do not promise live utility fulfilment, wallet refunds, payout automation, live rides or
  Pharmacy marketplace availability.
- Do not request passwords, OTPs, delivery codes, card details or provider credentials.
- Do not paste sensitive screenshots into Git-tracked documents.
- Use order/request references only where safe.
