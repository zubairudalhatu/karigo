# KariGO Design Specifications - Task 106

Date prepared: 2026-07-13

## Recommended Sizes

| Asset | Size |
| --- | --- |
| Instagram/Facebook square | 1080 x 1080 |
| Instagram/Facebook story/status | 1080 x 1920 |
| X post | 1600 x 900 |
| X banner | 1500 x 500 |
| LinkedIn post | 1200 x 1200 |
| LinkedIn banner | 1128 x 191 |
| WhatsApp flyer portrait | 1080 x 1350 |
| Website hero banner | 1920 x 800 |

## Logo Usage

- Use the official KariGO logo.
- Keep the logo clear.
- Do not stretch the logo.
- Maintain the red, black, white and light grey identity.
- Keep enough clear space around the logo.
- Avoid placing the logo on busy imagery unless a white/clean container is used.

## Colour Direction

- Red: primary CTA, highlight and brand energy.
- Black/charcoal: headline, strong contrast and navigation tone.
- White: clean background and app-first feel.
- Light grey: panels, cards and supporting sections.

## Typography Guidance

- Use bold headline treatment.
- Use a clean sans-serif style.
- Keep short line lengths on mobile/status graphics.
- Use strong CTA text.
- Maintain high contrast for readability.
- Avoid decorative fonts that reduce trust or clarity.

## Layout Guidance

- Design mobile-first.
- Prioritize one message per graphic.
- Keep CTA visible and button-like.
- Use simple service icons for food, groceries, parcel, SME Services and Delivery Captain.
- Leave whitespace around the logo and headline.
- Avoid crowded product collages.
- Do not use fake app-store badges or fake store links.

## Image And Screenshot Rules

- Do not show card details.
- Do not show OTPs or delivery code digits.
- Do not show passwords or login credentials.
- Do not show real customer, vendor or Delivery Captain private data.
- Do not use provider dashboard screenshots containing sensitive data.
- Use placeholder app screens where needed.

## Approved Service Labels

- Food delivery
- Groceries and market essentials
- Parcel delivery
- SME Services
- Delivery Captains

## Restricted Claims

- Do not say rides are live.
- Do not say utilities are live.
- Do not say wallet top-up or wallet withdrawal is live.
- Do not say automated payouts are live.
- Do not say full public launch unless the copy is clearly a future public launch teaser.
