# KariGO Soft Launch Video Prompt - Task 106

Date prepared: 2026-07-13

These are 10-second Google Gemini video prompts for concept generation. Do not mention
live rides or live utilities. Do not show card details, OTPs, passwords or payment
screenshots.

## 1. Customer-Focused Prompt

Scene description:

Create a clean 10-second mobile-first launch video set in Kano, Nigeria. Show a young
customer opening the KariGO app on a phone, browsing food, groceries and parcel delivery
icons, then a Delivery Captain moving through a simple route animation. Use KariGO red,
black, white and light grey. Include the official KariGO logo as a placeholder.

Nigerian/Kano context:

Use subtle Kano urban street cues, local shopfront energy and warm daylight. Keep it
modern and professional, not crowded.

Voice-over script:

Kano, KariGO is beginning controlled early access. Order food, groceries, market
essentials and parcels with selected pilot vendors.

On-screen text:

KariGO Early Access
Now starting in Kano

CTA:

Join Early Access

Safety guardrail:

Do not mention live rides, live utilities, wallet withdrawals, automated payouts or
Pharmacy marketplace.

## 2. Vendor-Focused Prompt

Scene description:

Create a 10-second video showing a Kano vendor receiving a KariGO order on a dashboard,
accepting it, preparing the package and marking it ready for pickup. Add a simple Delivery
Captain pickup motion line. Use KariGO red, black, white and light grey with clean
dashboard-inspired graphics.

Nigerian/Kano context:

Show a friendly local restaurant or market stall environment without showing real brand
names or private customer data.

Voice-over script:

Kano vendors, KariGO controlled early access is starting. Receive pilot orders, prepare
them clearly and connect with Delivery Captains.

On-screen text:

Vendors, join KariGO early access

CTA:

Become a Vendor

Safety guardrail:

Do not claim automated payout is live. Do not show bank details, payout screens, live
rides or live utilities.

## 3. Delivery Captain-Focused Prompt

Scene description:

Create a 10-second video showing a KariGO Delivery Captain opening the KariGO Captain App,
going online, receiving a delivery job, picking up a parcel from a vendor and completing a
safe customer handoff. Use route animation, red CTA accents and clean app-like cards.

Nigerian/Kano context:

Use Kano street movement and practical delivery energy. Keep the look trusted, fast and
community-driven.

Voice-over script:

Become a KariGO Delivery Captain. Help move pilot orders safely across Kano during
controlled early access.

On-screen text:

KariGO Captain
Delivery pilot in Kano

CTA:

Apply To Join

Safety guardrail:

Ride Captain is readiness-only during this phase. Live ride trips, live utilities and
automated payouts are not active.
