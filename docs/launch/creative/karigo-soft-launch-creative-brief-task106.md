# KariGO Soft Launch Creative Brief - Task 106

Date prepared: 2026-07-13

## 1. Campaign Title

KariGO Controlled Early Access - Kano Pilot

## 2. Campaign Goal

Invite selected customers, vendors and Delivery Captains into the controlled early access
pilot in Kano while keeping the message honest, local and readiness-safe.

## 3. Target Audiences

- Customers in Kano
- Food vendors and restaurants
- Grocery and market sellers
- Delivery Captains
- SMEs needing errand/service support
- Early partners and supporters

## 4. Brand Tone

- Modern
- Nigerian
- Trusted
- Fast
- Friendly
- Community-driven
- Professional

## 5. Visual Direction

Use KariGO brand colours:

- Red
- Black
- White
- Light grey

Use the official KariGO logo. Keep the logo clear and undistorted.

Visual style:

- Clean mobile-app-first layout
- Delivery movement
- Kano urban feel
- Vendor, customer and Delivery Captain connection
- Simple icons for food, groceries, parcel and SME Services
- Avoid cluttered product-heavy visuals
- Strong CTA button style

## 6. Key Message

KariGO is beginning controlled early access in Kano.

Core message:

KariGO connects customers, vendors and Delivery Captains for food, groceries, market
essentials, parcel delivery and SME Services.

## 7. CTA Options

- Join Early Access
- Order with KariGO
- Become a Vendor
- Become a Delivery Captain
- Get Early Access
- Partner with KariGO

## 8. Required Guardrails

- Do not claim rides are live.
- Do not claim utilities are live.
- Do not claim wallet top-up or wallet withdrawal is live.
- Do not claim automated payouts are live.
- Do not claim full nationwide launch.
- Do not show card details, OTPs, passwords or payment screenshots.
- Do not use misleading financial claims.
- Do not show provider dashboard screenshots containing sensitive data.
- Do not imply Pharmacy marketplace is active.

## Approved Copy Blocks

Short:

KariGO is beginning controlled early access in Kano.

Medium:

Food delivery, groceries and market essentials, parcel delivery and SME Services are
rolling out carefully with selected Kano users.

Long:

KariGO is beginning controlled early access in Kano, connecting customers, vendors and
Delivery Captains for everyday delivery needs while the team tests operations and service
quality carefully.
