# KariGO Final Pre-Pilot Activation Audit - Task 110

Date prepared: 2026-07-13
Pilot location: Kano
Pilot duration: 7 to 14 days
Selected initial payment mode: Mock payment

## Purpose

This document is the final audit checklist before inviting real controlled early access
participants into the Kano pilot.

This is a documentation and launch-operations gate only. It does not activate live
Paystack, Paystack Test Mode, live Accelerate.ng utilities, live Termii SMS, wallet
withdrawals, automatic refunds, live rides, ride dispatch, payouts, Pharmacy marketplace
or provider login.

## Critical Go Conditions

All of the following must be true before inviting real pilot users:

- Selected payment mode is mock payment.
- No open P0/P1 issue exists.
- APK links are approved and privately distributed only.
- Customers, vendors, Delivery Captains and operations admins are approved.
- Support and dispatch owners are online for the pilot window.
- Incident stop conditions are understood.
- No live provider or disabled feature is active.
- Management signs the final go/no-go record.
