## Invite Rules

- Invite only approved participants.
- Send access only through approved private channels.
- Do not include passwords in invite messages.
- Do not publish APK links publicly.
- Do not invite more than the approved pilot scope.
- Do not advertise as full public launch.
- Do not promise rides, utilities, wallet withdrawals, automated refunds, payouts or
  Pharmacy marketplace availability.

## Send Invite Decision

| Decision | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Send invites | All critical access and support checks pass |
| Send staged invites | Send to a smaller first batch only |
| Hold invites | Missing approval, support, APK, domain, access-control or guardrail check |
| Cancel invites | Security, privacy or live-provider risk |
