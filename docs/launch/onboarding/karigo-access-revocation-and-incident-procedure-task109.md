# KariGO Access Revocation And Incident Procedure - Task 109

Date prepared: 2026-07-13

## Purpose

Define how KariGO should suspend or revoke participant access during the controlled Kano
pilot when access is no longer approved or an incident occurs.

## Revocation Triggers

Revoke or suspend access when:

- a participant shares APK/access links publicly;
- login details, OTPs, delivery codes or screenshots with private data are shared;
- participant attempts to access an unauthorized surface;
- vendor, customer or Delivery Captain data appears exposed;
- a participant leaves the pilot;
- management pauses or stops the pilot;
- a P0/P1 security, privacy or operational issue affects that access path.

## Procedure

| Step | Owner | Action | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Support Lead | Record issue with safe participant ID only | Incident ID |
| 2 | Pilot Lead | Decide suspend or revoke | Decision note |
| 3 | Technical/Admin Lead | Disable or restrict access where supported | Completion note |
| 4 | Operations Lead | Notify participant through approved channel | Message sent yes/no |
| 5 | QA Lead | Confirm no sensitive data is stored in Git evidence | Confirmation |
| 6 | Pilot Lead | Decide continue, pause or stop affected pilot flow | Decision log |

## Participant Notification Template

KariGO has paused your pilot access while we review an operational issue. Please do not
use or share pilot app links until KariGO confirms the next step. Do not send passwords,
OTPs, delivery codes or private screenshots in response.

## Restore Access Criteria

Access may be restored only when:

- the issue is understood;
- any exposed link/data risk is contained;
- the participant confirms pilot rules;
- Pilot Lead approves restoration;
- QA records a safe retest or verification note.
