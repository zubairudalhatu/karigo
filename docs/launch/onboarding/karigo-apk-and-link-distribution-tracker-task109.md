## APK Distribution Rules

- Share only approved release-candidate APK links.
- Do not forward unapproved builds.
- Do not upload APKs to public drives or websites.
- Do not send login credentials with APK links.
- Do not share links in public WhatsApp groups, social media or website pages.
- If a link is shared wrongly, pause distribution and follow the revocation procedure.
