# Post-Launch 30-Day Review Plan

## Timeline

- Day 1: uptime, provider, login, payment, dispatch, support, safety, and critical incidents.
- Day 3: order completion, rider/vendor capacity, complaints/refunds, early fixes.
- Day 7: customer growth/repeat intent, operational SLA, finance reconciliation, risks.
- Day 14: trend and cohort review, partner performance, support load, expansion readiness.
- Day 30: full performance, finance, product, technical, legal/incident, and strategy review.

## Metrics

- Customer growth
- Order growth
- Completion rate
- Repeat orders
- Vendor performance
- Rider performance
- Support tickets
- Refund rate
- Technical issues
- Revenue and GMV
- Promo usage

## Day-30 Decision

- [ ] Continue current city scope
- [ ] Add vendors/riders
- [ ] Expand zones/service categories
- [ ] Improve product before expansion
- [ ] Pause/reduce expansion
- [ ] Prepare next city

Record evidence, conditions, owners, budget/capacity, risk acceptance, and rollback
triggers. Do not add service categories or cities solely because 30 days elapsed.
