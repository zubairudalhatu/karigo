# Post-Soft-Launch Review Plan

## Purpose

Evaluate the controlled Kano pilot across product, technical, operational, financial,
vendor, rider, customer, and support outcomes. The review determines whether KariGO
should continue or extend the pilot, prepare for public launch, pause for fixes, or
rework selected areas.

## Review Gates

- Day 7: early reliability, safety, support capacity, and critical-incident review.
- Day 14: trend, repeat usage, vendor/rider reliability, finance, and scalability review.
- Pilot end (recommended week 2 to 4): final evidence and public-launch recommendation.

## Review Areas

- Product performance
- Order completion
- Vendor reliability
- Rider reliability
- Customer satisfaction
- Support performance
- Payment and refund stability
- Admin control
- Technical stability
- Financial visibility
- Operational scalability

## Review Method

Use daily reports, KPI definitions, incident/feedback logs, support tickets, admin
reports, reconciliation records, and the pilot performance template. Every finding must
have evidence, severity, owner, due date, and launch impact.

## Decision

The review panel records one decision: continue pilot, expand pilot, prepare for public
launch, pause and fix blockers, or rebuild selected areas. Public launch still requires
the separate go/no-go meeting and completed readiness checklist.
