# Controlled Expansion Criteria

Expansion requires a documented Management decision after reviewing daily/weekly
evidence. It is never automatic.

## Required Conditions

- Completion rate meets the approved floor and critical incidents are closed.
- Vendor rejection/delay rates are within target.
- Rider availability and delivery performance support current plus proposed demand.
- Support volume/resolution and refund disputes are controlled.
- No unresolved critical technical, security, payment, or legal issue exists.
- Admin monitoring, daily reports, staffing, and escalation remain effective.

## Decisions

- [ ] Continue current pilot unchanged
- [ ] Expand to more vendors
- [ ] Expand to more zones
- [ ] Invite more customers
- [ ] Pause expansion
- [ ] Reduce/roll back/pause pilot

For every expansion, record evidence, new limits, responsible owners, review date, and
rollback triggers. Expand one dimension at a time where practical.
