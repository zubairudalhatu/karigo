# Pilot Governance

## Roles

- **Management Lead:** approves scope, reviews daily reports, decides continue/expand/pause.
- **Operations Lead:** runs daily operations, coordinates vendors/riders, owns operational closure.
- **Dispatch Officer:** monitors ready orders, assigns/reassigns riders, handles delivery delays.
- **Support Officer:** manages tickets/complaints and escalates unresolved issues.
- **Finance Officer:** reviews payments/refunds, settlements, rider earnings, and reconciliation.
- **Technical Lead:** monitors API/apps, triages bugs/incidents, deploys approved fixes/rollback.
- **Marketing Lead:** controls invitations/messages/promo usage and gathers feedback.

## Decision Rhythm

- Shift opening check: staffing, zones, vendor/rider availability, system/provider status.
- Live operations: escalation matrix and incident log.
- Daily close: KPI/report/finance/incident review and action assignment.
- Weekly management gate: continue, restrict, expand, pause, or end pilot.

No one role may unilaterally approve real provider activation, expansion, refunds outside
policy, or production changes without the required documented approvals.
