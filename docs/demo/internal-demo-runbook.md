# Internal Demo Runbook

## Part A: System Overview

Explain KariGO's MVP purpose, four product surfaces, staging/synthetic-data boundary,
and that payment, OTP/SMS, email, WhatsApp, push, and payouts remain mocked.

## Part B: Customer Journey

1. Log in as demo customer and show profile/address.
2. Browse vendor/products; add items to cart.
3. Validate `KARIGOFIRST`, create order, initiate and verify mock payment.
4. Show order tracking and delivery OTP warning.

## Part C: Vendor Journey

1. Log in as demo vendor and open the paid order.
2. Accept, mark preparing, then ready for pickup.

## Part D: Admin Journey

1. Log in as admin; show dashboard/live orders.
2. Open dispatch board and assign the available demo rider.

## Part E: Rider Journey

1. Log in as demo rider, go online, and accept the job.
2. Progress through pickup, on-the-way, arrival, and delivered.
3. Complete with the customer delivery OTP.

## Part F: Support And Reports

1. Create a customer support ticket; reply/resolve from admin.
2. Show reports, vendor settlement, rider earning, and notifications.

## Part G: Management Discussion

Review what is ready, what remains mocked, staging findings, critical launch blockers,
provider go-live decisions, operational staffing, legal/security approvals, and assigned
next actions. Use `demo-feedback-form.md` to record the decision.
